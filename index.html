Welcome to our Mobile App FitNomadic

FitNomadic is a gym app that lets you find and book trainers, access video workouts, and customize meal plans. It offers a comprehensive platform for managing your fitness journey.

Here are 3 key points for a "Booking to Trainer" feature:

Customer:

Book Sessions:
Search and book trainers based on availability and preferences
View and manage upcoming and past bookings

2. Trainer:

Manage Bookings:
View and manage upcoming and past bookings
Update booking status (e.g., Accept, Reject)

Here are 3 key points for a "Meals" feature:

Add Meals:

Allow users to add their own meals or select from a database of pre-existing meals
Input fields for meal name, ingredients, calories, macronutrients, and tags (e.g., vegan, gluten-free)

2. Customize Meals:

Enable users to customize meals based on their dietary needs and preferences
Adjustable serving sizes and ingredient quantities

Here are the details you can view about a trainer in the FitNomadic app:

Trainer Details:

Booking Amount: View the trainer's booking fee
Rating: Check the trainer's overall rating based on user reviews
Rating: Check the trainer's overall rating based on user reviews
Experience: View the trainer's experience level (e.g., beginner, intermediate, advanced)
Qualifications: Check the trainer's certifications and qualifications
Services Offered: See what services the trainer provides (e.g., personal training, group classes, nutrition planning)
Availability: View the trainer's schedule and availability
Reviews: Read reviews and feedback from other users who have worked with the trainer
Profile Picture: View the trainer's profile picture
Specialization: See the trainer's area of expertise (e.g., weightlifting, yoga, cardio)

Live chat when you needed

Trainer Side

Type a message or send an image/video to the customer
Attach files (images, videos, documents) from your device or take a new photo/video
Preview and edit images before sending (e.g., annotate, crop)
Type a message or send an image/video to the customer

Customer Side

Receive images/videos from the trainer with a preview thumbnail
View full-size images/videos with zoom and download options
Preview and edit images before sending (e.g., annotate, crop)
React with emojis or send a quick response (e.g., "Thanks!", "Got it!")
